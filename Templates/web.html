A Predictive Framework for Chronic Kidney Disease Using Machine Learning Algorithms

Chronic kidney disease (CKD) or Chronic Renal Disease (CRD) prediction using machine learning involves leveraging algorithms to analyze medical data and identify patterns indicative of CKD. By training models on patient data, such as lab results and medical history, machine learning can predict the likelihood of a patient developing CKD. This proactive approach allows for early diagnosis and intervention, potentially improving patient outcomes. Utilizing machine learning in CKD prediction can also help optimize treatment plans and resource allocation.
